"Cybertron Vault" Project information

  • [Category] Web Design; Visual Identity; UI Design
  • [Date] June 2026
  • [Software] Figma; Adobe Illustrator; Adobe Photoshop
  • [Type of Project] Personal

Cybertron Vault is a conceptual ecommerce platform designed for Transformers collectors. The project focused on creating a premium shopping experience through immersive product presentation, intuitive navigation, and streamlined product discovery. Alongside the website, I developed a cohesive visual identity, including the logo, color palette, and typography, to reinforce the brand's premium and futuristic aesthetic.


Responsive layouts for tablet and mobile are currently in development and will be added in a future update.
