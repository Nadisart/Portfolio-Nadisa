"Mirtila" Project information

  • Category: Branding; Illustration; Packaging; 3D Modelling and Painting; Visual Identity Design; Ux/Ui; Web Design
  • Project date: December 2023 - March 2024
  • Software: Adobe Illustrator; Adobe Photoshop; Adobe InDesign; Womp; Figma; Google Forms
  • Type of Project: Academic

Inspired by the growing trend of bubble tea cafes and the desire to create an inclusive environment, I conceptualized Mirtila Bubble Tea Cafe's branding and visual identity. This fictional cafe is designed to welcome all customers while promoting sustainability and compassion. With a charming alien mascot named Mirtila leading the way, the branding captures the whimsical essence of the cafe's mission. Mirtila, a playful twist on the Portuguese word for blueberry, 'mirtilo,' symbolizes the cafe's commitment to freshness and natural ingredients. Through this project, I explored the intersection of creativity and social responsibility, crafting a brand identity that embodies both.


The color palette of pink and green used in Mirtila's branding is inspired by Mirtila's own appearance, with her skin color and hair serving as the basis. Green symbolizes nature and veganism, reflecting the cafe's commitment to sustainability and plant-based offerings. Pink, on the other hand, represents a sweet, comfortable, and friendly ambiance, inviting customers to relax and enjoy their time at the cafe. Mirtila's personal story adds depth to the brand, as she has always felt different and struggled to find acceptance. In response, she created her cafe as a haven where anyone can feel valued and embraced for who they are, free from judgment and discrimination.


For the typography, I selected 'Baby Doll' as the institutional font for its playful and soft appearance, perfectly complementing Mirtila's inviting atmosphere. Its rounded shapes and gentle curves convey a sense of friendliness and warmth, enhancing the overall brand identity. As a complementary typeface for longer body text, I opted for 'Friend,' which offers increased legibility while maintaining a round and friendly aesthetic. Together, these fonts contribute to a cohesive visual identity that aligns with Mirtila's inclusive and welcoming ethos. The brand's icons are a taiyaki, blueberries, a dumpling, a slice of japanese shortcake, a peach, cherries, a heart, a cup of bubble tea and onigiri.
