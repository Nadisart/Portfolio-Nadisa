"Nostalgia" Project information

  • Category: Editorial Design
  • Project date: May 2024 - June 2024
  • Software: Adobe Illustrator; Adobe Indesign; Adobe Photoshop
  • Type of project: Academic

For my editorial design class I had to create a multidisciplinary magazine, I decided to create a magazine about nostalgia focused on videogames, anime and y2k culture. I choose an 8 column grid and the size of 230 x 280 mm for the layout. I also did 2 alternative covers.
